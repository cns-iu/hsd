Health System Dynamics

Informatics for Integrating Biology and the Bedside (i2b2) data is organized by the i2b2 ontology. The SCILHS public i2b2 ontology has 490,484 concepts under root path \PCORI and is 11 levels deep and can be found at github: https://github.com/SCILHS/scilhs-ontology/tree/master/Ontology.


This interactive Health System Dynamics ontology visualization enables i2b2 administrators and others to see the overall structure of the ontology, to examine (in)activate paths, hide branches that are not relevant, and facilitates ontology comparisons. The visualization also enables investigators to locate specific codes/diagnosis, and helps determine the breadth of the data.
